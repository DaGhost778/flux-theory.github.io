Flux Theory

David Quintarelli works with sound, interaction, and generative systems.
He develops audiovisual environments using Max/MSP and TouchDesigner, creating installations, performances, and custom tools.
He also works as an audio and video technician for live shows and theatre, and teaches live electronics, synthesis, and audio production.
His artistic practice explores sound, image, and interactive systems, blending physical and digital dimensions.
